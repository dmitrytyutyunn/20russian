Übung macht den Meister

This platform introduces the material which will enable you to learn basic structures of the Russian language. This platform is based on the technique of 20 hours. This technique was developed in order to guarantee the efficient diving into the Russian language. The key element of this platform is the memorization hints for russian words. Just press the russian word and you will see it.

Besides there are different applications which will enable you to check your vocabulary and also to polish grammatical structures. We did our best in order to make your learning process as enjoable as possible.
